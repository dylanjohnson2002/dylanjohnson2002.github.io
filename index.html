About Me

Hello, my name is Dylan Johnson, and I'm currently embracing the exciting world of gaming and interactive media in my second year at Boise State's GIMM program. With a burning passion for game design, I am enthusiastically diving into the intricacies of creating engaging and immersive gaming experiences. Outside the digital realm, I find joy and fulfillment in my job at a local pet store, where I dedicate my time to caring for animals. This blend of technological creativity and a love for animals defines who I am and continuously shapes my journey both personally and professionally.
